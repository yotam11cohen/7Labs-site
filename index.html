How It Works

From Hair to
Health Intelligence

Four precise steps transform a simple hair sample into a comprehensive metabolic timeline spanning years of your personal health history.

01

Sample Collection

A small hair sample from the nape of your neck — collected at home with no specialist required. Each centimeter of hair represents approximately one month of your metabolic history, meaning a 7 cm sample reveals 7 months of biochemical data.

02

Precision Preparation

Our certified lab segments your hair sample into precise 1 cm sections using a standardized SOP, each numbered sequentially from root to tip. This preserves the full chronological record of your metabolic history — with a 100% sample processing success rate across all clinical studies.

03

Dual-Platform Analysis

Two complementary LC-MS/MS platforms — HILIC for polar compounds (amino acids, cortisol, endocannabinoids) and HSST3 for hydrophobic compounds (lipids, surfactants, pharmaceutical traces) — together profile 3,806+ metabolic features with <5 ppm mass accuracy.

04

Personalized Report

Advanced bioinformatics and proprietary ML algorithms apply patient-specific baseline normalization to identify your unique stress biomarker patterns, reversible signatures, and temporal drift indicators — translated into clear, actionable health intelligence.

Everything you need to know before getting started with hair metabolomics.

Hair metabolomics analyzes over 3,806 chemical compounds embedded in your hair shaft — including cortisol (the primary stress hormone), sex hormones (testosterone, progesterone), endocannabinoids (2-AG, AEA), amino acids, and environmental exposures. Each centimeter stores approximately one month of your metabolic history, providing a longitudinal picture that a single blood test cannot offer.

After signing up, we’ll mail you a collection kit with detailed instructions. Cut a small sample from the nape of your neck (about the thickness of a pencil), place it in the provided envelope, and mail it back to our certified lab. The entire collection takes less than 5 minutes at home, and no specialist is needed.

Hair grows approximately 1 centimeter per month. A 12 cm sample reveals 12 months of your metabolic history. In our clinical studies, subjects provided samples up to 60 cm long, enabling analysis going back several years. The length of your hair sample determines the time window we can analyze.

Hair metabolomics is a preventive health and wellness service. Most insurance plans do not currently cover this type of comprehensive metabolomic profiling. We offer transparent, direct-pay pricing and can provide documentation for potential HSA/FSA reimbursement. Contact us for current pricing details.
